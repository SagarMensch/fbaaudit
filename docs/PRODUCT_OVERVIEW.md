## The Solution

### Intelligent Automation Platform

Our platform transforms freight invoice processing through four core capabilities:

#### 1. **Multi-Channel Ingestion**
Automatically capture invoice data from any source:
- **PDF OCR**: 98% extraction accuracy using Vision AI
- **EDI Integration**: X12 (210, 810) and EDIFACT support
- **Email Parsing**: Automatic attachment extraction
- **API Connectivity**: Real-time carrier integrations

#### 2. **Intelligent Audit Engine**
Validate every invoice against multiple data points:
- **3-Way Match**: Invoice ↔ Purchase Order ↔ Proof of Delivery
- **Contract Rate Validation**: Automatic rate card lookup
- **Parcel Audit**: Small package optimization (GSR, Dim Weight)
- **Anomaly Detection**: AI-powered fraud and duplicate detection

#### 3. **India Tax Compliance**
Built-in tax engine for Indian regulations:
- **GST Calculation**: Forward Charge (FCM) and Reverse Charge (RCM)
- **TDS Computation**: Section 194C compliance
- **GSTIN Validation**: Real-time verification
- **Tax Reporting**: Ready-to-file formats

#### 4. **Automated Settlement**
Streamline payment processing:
- **GL Coding**: AI-predicted account allocation
- **Batch Generation**: Automated payment runs
- **ERP Integration**: SAP, Oracle, D365 connectivity
- **Audit Trail**: Complete payment history
